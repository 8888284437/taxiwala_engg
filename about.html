Story of Engineer Taxiwala

Moving billion dreams, One ride at a time.

🚖 Engineer Taxiwala: A Story of Passion, Hard Work & Trust
Every great journey starts with a single step, and mine began in 2014-15, watching my father drive a taxi for another company. He worked tirelessly, ensuring passengers reached their destinations safely. But deep down, I knew we could build something of our own—something bigger, something better.
I encouraged my father to buy our own car and start our own taxi business. At first, he was hesitant, uncertain whether it would work. But in 2016, we took a leap of faith and purchased our first T-Permit taxi. From that moment, we never looked back.
As the business grew, I too stepped in while pursuing my Diploma in Engineering. In 2018, alongside my studies, I started driving part-time, balancing college and work. That same year, we bought our second vehicle—an Innova, expanding our service.
By 2021, I had completed my Computer Engineering degree, and like most graduates, I stepped into the corporate world, working in an MNC for a year. But deep down, I knew my true calling wasn’t behind a desk—it was on the roads, building something extraordinary. In 2022, I left my job and dedicated myself full-time to Engineer Taxiwala.
From One Taxi to a Fleet of 158 🚖
What started as one car is now a trusted taxi service managing 158 vehicles, including:
✅ Sedan (4+1) – Comfortable city rides
✅ MUV (6+1) – Spacious family trips
✅ SUV (7+1) – Perfect for long journeys
✅ Tempo Traveller (13 to 50 seater) – Group travel made easy
With every ride, we focus on what truly matters: clean and well-maintained taxis, professional service, corporate expertise, punctuality, and helpful drivers who make every journey memorable.
